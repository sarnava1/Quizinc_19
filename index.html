About Us

QuizInc, the official quizzing club of NIT Durgapur, provides an incredible interactive platform for all those who are endlessly curious and love exchanging ideas and knowledge. It is indeed the "Knowledge Club" of NIT Durgapur.
A club which remains active throughout the year, QuizInc hosts a variety of quizzes every month. From collaborating with other NITs, to holding interesting events, to witnessing participants from reputed colleges all over Kolkata and Durgapur, QuizInc does it all.

Quizzitch Cup

While QuizInc organizes quizzes all year, all of them culminate into this huge extravaganza of quizzing, knowledge and a healthy dose (read: overdose) of fun! With the advent of the new decade, we are absolutely pumped about the first big event of 2020: Quizzitch Cup!
A festival full of relentless information, knowledge and fun, Quizzitch Cup 2020 is a celebration of an activity we all love: quizzing. With a huge array of quizzes lined up to be hosted by QuizInc and attended by schools and colleges from all over the Eastern region of India, it just doesn't get any better!

  • DC Vs Marvel Quiz

    Don your favourite superhero T shirt, and amaze everyone with your superpowers, just like the students at the Xavier's School for Gifted Youngsters. From Natasha Romanoff to Barry Allen, no one will be spared in this quiz. The Crossover no one wanted, but everyone needs.
    The DC-Marvel Quiz. The Endgame.

  • Mela Quiz

    A quiz that spans from Kurosawa to Kashyap, from Byomkesh Bakshi to Hercule Poirot, from Raja Ravi Verma to Jackson Pollock-whatever be your interest. Movies. Entertainment. Literature. Art. Pick your poison this MELA quiz!

  • Sports Quiz

    A quiz for people who conduct play-by-play analysis of every match, and a quiz for people who fall asleep and catch up on the highlights. A quiz for the people who've spent hundreds on Hotstar subscriptions, and a quiz for people who've spent thousands to see RCB lose. A quiz for one and all.
    The Sports Quiz.

  • Qureka General Quiz

    With no specific topic, this quiz is both for the uninitiated as well as the veterans. With questions on all subjects under the sun, even a library fitting enough to be in Belle's sweetest dreams falls short for this seemingly common, yet very different quiz!
    Qureka. The common man's (or woman's) quiz.

  • Qmanji

    Are you the friend who everyone consults when they need to visit some quaint "undiscovered" place in India? From Incredible India to the locales of Durgapur A-Zone, QuizInc presents to you Q-Manji, the official India Quiz themed treasure hunt!
